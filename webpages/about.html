This version of RPI Petitions is the collaborative effort of members of the Rensselaer Center for Open Source (RCOS) and Student Senate.


Curious about creating a new petition? Here are some steps to help get you started.

1. Have an idea.

Search for petitions that match your concerns. If a petition for your issue already exists, sign it! This will help raise support for your cause and reach the threshold of 250 signatures (as of 03/13/2015) to gain Senate support.


The Rensselaer Union Student Senate empowers the Rules and Elections Committee and Web Technologies Group with moderating privileges on the RPI Petitions website.
